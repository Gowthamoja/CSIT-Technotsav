Welcome to Technotsav 2025-CSI

The ultimate tech fest at Sri Indu College of Engineering & Technology!


Join us for a series of exciting events that will showcase innovation, creativity, and skills in the fields of technology and beyond. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or someone looking to have fun, Technotsav 2025 has something for everyone.

Technical Events

Our technical events are designed for all the innovators, coders, and tech enthusiasts who want to showcase their skills and creativity. Register now and get ready to compete with the best!

  • Paper Presentation - Present your research or innovations in the field of technology.
  • Tech Quiz - Test your knowledge and prove your expertise in the world of tech.
  • Code RefleX - Compete in coding challenges and see how fast you can solve problems.
  • Just a Prompt - Create innovative solutions to unique coding prompts in a limited time.

Non-Technical Events

Our non-technical events are just as exciting, offering fun and engaging activities for all participants. Don't miss out on these entertaining challenges!

  • Treasure Hunt - Follow the clues to uncover hidden treasures in this thrilling event.
  • Heads Up - A fun guessing game that will have you and your team thinking fast!
  • Flip Toe - A creative twist on the classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe.
  • Balloon Hunt - Test your agility and teamwork as you race to find the hidden balloons!
